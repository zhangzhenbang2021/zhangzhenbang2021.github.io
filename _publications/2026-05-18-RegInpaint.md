We propose *RegInpaint*, a recovery framework that jointly addresses deformation correction and missing-information restoration caused by Supporting Film Folding degradation in serial section electron microscopy. RegInpaint formulates recovery as a joint problem of 3D elastic registration and image inpainting, improving restoration quality and downstream neuron segmentation accuracy across four electron microscopy datasets.
