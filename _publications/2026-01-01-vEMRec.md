<sup>‡</sup> Equal contribution.

This paper presents *vEMRec*, an end-to-end 3D alignment pipeline for volume electron microscopy. The method is validated on six real-world datasets exceeding 1 TB, demonstrating improved ultrastructural recovery and processing efficiency for connectome-scale analysis.
